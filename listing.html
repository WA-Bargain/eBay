About us

What is Driven?

Driven is a company that makes buying and selling a car as fuss free as possible. For the owner, we handle everything from cleaning the car, to listing it and handling enquiries and test drives. For potential buyers, we provide peace of mind through our AA inspections and we try to provide you with all the information you need about the car to make an informed buying decision.

Are you car dealers?

No. We do not buy cars, we only help owners sell their cars.

Can you help me sell my car?

Of course, provided you (and the car) are based in London, and the car is worth more than £5,000. Visit our website (www.driven-cars.com) for more details.

Buying the car

Can I see the AA inspection report?

Absolutely. Once you have enquired about the car to us, we will send you an electronic copy of the inspection report.

Can I see the car on a different day to the test drive day?

Unfortunately, no. At Driven we try to make buying a selling a car as fuss free for everyone. This means that we minimise the disruption to the owner of the car, and the best way to do this is to have all the test drives happen on the same day.

If I want to buy the car, how do I agree a price?

How do I pay for the car?

If we agree a price for you to buy the car, you will transfer the money direct to the owner of the car. Once they have received the money, we will arrange for you to collect the car.
